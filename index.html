Account & Order History

How can I view my order history?
Just log in and go to "My Orders". Everything's neatly listed there.
Can I cancel my order after placing it?
Yes, you can cancel your order as long as it hasn't been delivered.
Is there a time limit for cancelling an order?
Orders can typically be cancelled within the delivery status of placement.
Can I cancel part of my order?
Yes, if your order contains multiple items, you can cancel individual items before they are shipped.

Offers & Loyalty

Do you have a loyalty program or rewards?
We don’t have any rewards at the moment.
Are there any ongoing promotions?
We don’t have any active promotions right now, but great deals are always around the corner.
Can I gift wrap my order?
Yes, gift wrapping is available for select items at checkout.

Order Management

My order has already shipped. Can I still cancel it?
Unfortunately, shipped orders cannot be cancelled. You can initiate a return instead.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, if it hasn’t been delivered yet. Go to the 'Main Menu,' select 'Cancel,' choose the item, provide a reason, and follow the instructions.
How do I modify my order?
Contact support immediately. Changes are only possible before shipping.
What happens if I ordered the wrong item?
You can cancel or return it once delivered. Contact support for help.
Can I add items to an existing order?
No, but you can place a new order. Bundled checkout options are coming soon.
Will I be charged for cancellations?
No cancellation fee if done before shipping. After shipping, return policies apply.
