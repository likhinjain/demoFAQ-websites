Frequently asked questions

Click a question to view the answer.

Account & order history

How can I view my order history?
Log in and open “My Orders” to see all past and current orders in one place.
Can I cancel my order after placing it?
Yes — you can cancel as long as the order hasn’t been delivered. If shipped, initiate a return.
Is there a time limit for cancelling an order?
Cancellation is typically allowed until the order moves beyond processing. Exact windows may vary by product.
Can I cancel part of my order?
Yes. For multi‑item orders, you can cancel individual items before they are shipped.

Offers & loyalty

Do you have a loyalty program or rewards?
Not at the moment. Stay tuned — perks and rewards may be introduced in future.
Are there any ongoing promotions?
No active promotions right now, but new deals are added regularly.
Can I gift wrap my order?
Yes, gift wrapping is available for select items during checkout.

Order management

My order has already shipped. Can I still cancel it?
Shipped orders can’t be cancelled, but you can create a return request after delivery.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes. Go to Main Menu → Cancel → choose item → provide a reason → confirm to cancel.
How do I modify my order?
Contact support immediately. Modifications are only possible before shipping.
What happens if I ordered the wrong item?
You can cancel before shipping or return/exchange after delivery. Support can guide you.
Can I add items to an existing order?
Not to the same order. Place a new order. Bundled checkout options may come later.
Will I be charged for cancellations?
No fee if cancelled before shipping. After shipping, standard return policies apply.

Order status & tracking

How long does it take to process an order?
Most orders process within 24–48 hours. Peak times may introduce delays.
Why was my order cancelled?
Common reasons: payment failure, stock issues, or address problems. A notification is sent with details.
My order shows delivered but I haven’t received it. What should I do?
Contact a live agent. We’ll investigate and resolve it quickly.
Why hasn’t my tracking number updated yet?
Tracking can take up to 24 hours to update after dispatch.
Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?
Address changes are possible only before shipping. Reach out to live support.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
Cards, UPI, wallets, and net banking are accepted.
Is it safe to enter my card details online?
Yes. Payments are protected with secure encryption and industry‑standard practices.

Returns, refund & exchanges

What is your return policy?
Eligible items can be returned within 7 days of delivery unless specified otherwise on the product page.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Refunds typically take 5–7 business days after the returned item is received.
Will I be charged for return shipping?
Return shipping is free for eligible items. Some categories may have a small fee.
What if I received a damaged or wrong item?
Connect with a live agent. We’ll assist with replacement or a refund.

Shipping & delivery

Do you ship internationally?
Yes, international shipping is available for select items.
Can I schedule a delivery time?
For eligible locations, choose a preferred delivery slot at checkout.
What if my package is lost or damaged?
Contact support immediately. We’ll investigate and arrange a replacement or refund.
